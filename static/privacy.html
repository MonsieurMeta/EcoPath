Privacy Policy

Welcome to the EcoPath Privacy Policy! Just like a secret garden, we keep your information safe and sound. By using EcoPath, you trust us with your data, and we’re more protective of it than a mother hen with her chicks.

1. Information We Collect

We gather a bit of your data to make EcoPath as useful as a Swiss Army knife—but for eco-living! Here’s what we scoop up:

  • Location Data: We track where you walk, but not in a creepy way. We’re just here to cheer on every step you take towards reducing your carbon footprint.
  • Camera Access: We’ll peek through your camera only to help identify recyclable materials and assess your garden’s health—think of us as your personal eco-assistant.
  • Account Details: Username and password—you know, the usual suspects so we can keep in touch and send you eco-tips and updates.

    • 2. How We Use Your Data

    Your data helps us help you. It’s a symbiotic relationship, like bees and flowers:

  • Personalization: To offer advice as tailored as a bespoke suit made of recycled fabrics.
  • Improvement: We analyze data to enhance EcoPath, ensuring it’s as efficient as a well-composted garden.
  • Communication: To contact you with news about your eco-journey, but we promise not to spam—only organic, non-GMO emails from us!

    • 3. Sharing Your Information

    We don’t share your data with third parties, because nobody likes a gossip.

    4. Data Security

    Securing your data is a top priority, akin to locking up a bicycle in the city. We use industry-standard security measures, even though we sometimes wish we could use a dragon-guarded fortress.

    5. Your Rights

    You’ve got the power to control your data:

  • Access and Update: Peek at your data anytime, like checking on seedlings in a sprout tray. If something’s off, you can update it to keep everything fresh and accurate.
  • Delete: If you decide to leave us (which would make us as sad as a wilted plant), you can request to delete your data. We’ll handle it with the care of disposing of a biodegradable pot.

    • 6. Changes to This Policy

    Our Privacy Policy may grow and change, like a tree through the seasons. We’ll let you know about any updates, as clearly as explaining why leaves turn colors in the fall.

    7. Contact Us

    If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or need tips on eco-friendly living, don’t hesitate to reach out. We’re here to help, not just with your data but with your whole eco-lifestyle!
